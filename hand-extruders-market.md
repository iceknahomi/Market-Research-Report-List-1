<p><p>Hand extruders are specialized devices used for the extrusion of materials, primarily in small-scale operations. The market consists of two main types: twin screw hand extruders and single screw hand extruders. Twin screw hand extruders utilize two intermeshing screws for better mixing and material processing, making them suitable for complex formulations. In contrast, single screw hand extruders consist of one screw, providing a simpler design for basic extrusion tasks. Both types cater to varying industrial needs, influencing market growth and applications.</p></p>
