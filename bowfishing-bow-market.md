<p><p>Bowfishing bows are categorized into three main types: recurve, compound, and lever bows. Recurve bows are traditional and provide simplicity, making them easy to handle in various environments. Compound bows feature a system of pulleys that enhances accuracy and reduces draw weight, appealing to those seeking performance. Lever bows utilize a lever mechanism for easier cocking and firing, offering a unique blend of power and ease of use. Each type caters to different skill levels and preferences among bowfishing enthusiasts.</p></p>
