<p><p>The horizontal kneader market can be categorized into dynamic and static types based on their operational mechanisms. Dynamic horizontal kneaders feature rotating blades that continuously mix and knead materials, enhancing efficiency and ensuring consistent results. In contrast, static horizontal kneaders rely on fixed blades, with the mixing occurring due to the movement of the materials themselves. Both types are utilized in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and polymers, catering to different mixing requirements and material properties.</p></p>
