<p><p>The Direct Heat Metering System market includes two primary types: Evaporating Style Heat Cost Allocators and Electric Heat Cost Allocators. Evaporating Style allocators measure heat consumption based on the evaporation of a liquid, providing accurate readings for billing and energy efficiency. Electric Heat Cost Allocators, on the other hand, utilize electrical sensors to track heat usage, offering a more direct measurement approach. Both types aim to enhance energy accountability and optimize heating costs in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
