<p><p>The 2.5D interposer market is primarily categorized into silicon, organic, and glass types. Silicon interposers, known for their high performance and thermal conductivity, facilitate advanced chip packaging. Organic interposers, while offering cost-effectiveness and lighter weight, often trade off some electrical performance. Glass interposers provide excellent signal integrity and lower dielectric losses, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Each type serves different application needs, influencing their adoption based on factors like performance requirements, production costs, and technological advancements.</p></p>
