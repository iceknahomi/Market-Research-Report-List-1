<p><p>The Aerospace and Automotive MRAM market includes two primary types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM utilizes a magnetic toggle switch mechanism to store data, offering high speed and low power consumption, ideal for automotive safety applications. STT-MRAM, or Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM, leverages spin-polarized currents for data writing, providing higher density and better scalability. Both types cater to the growing demand for reliable, non-volatile memory solutions in electronic control systems and advanced driver-assistance systems in these sectors.</p></p>
