<p><p>The digital textile printing dyes market encompasses various ink types tailored for specific fabrics and applications. Acid inks are primarily used for protein fibers like silk and wool, offering vibrant colors. Disperse inks target synthetic fibers, providing excellent color fastness. Dye-based inks are versatile for various textiles, while reactive inks bond chemically with natural fibers, ensuring durability and brilliance. Sublimation inks are ideal for polyester fabrics, transforming into gas for deep, permanent dyeing. Each type enhances textile printing versatility and quality.</p></p>
