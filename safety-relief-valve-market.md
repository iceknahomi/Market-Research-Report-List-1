<p><p>The safety relief valve market includes various types, primarily the spring-type and lever-type relief valves. Spring-type relief valves utilize a mechanical spring to maintain pressure, automatically opening to release excess pressure when it exceeds a preset limit, ensuring system safety. In contrast, lever-type relief valves rely on a lever mechanism which allows manual adjustment and operation, offering more control in certain applications. Both types play critical roles in protecting equipment and maintaining safety in industrial processes.</p></p>
