<p><strong>The Brass Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting-Rolling ( CR )</li><li>Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brass tube market can be categorized into three main types based on manufacturing processes: casting, rolling (CR), and extrusion. </p><p>Casting involves pouring molten brass into molds to create complex shapes, providing excellent dimensional accuracy. Rolling (CR) processes involve thickness reduction through mechanical means, resulting in flexible, lightweight tubes ideal for various applications. Extrusion involves forcing heated brass through a die to produce continuous shapes, offering uniform thickness and high strength. Each type serves distinct industry needs, balancing performance, cost, and design flexibility.</p></p>
