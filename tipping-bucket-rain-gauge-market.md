<p><p>A Tipping Bucket Rain Gauge is a meteorological instrument used to measure the amount of liquid precipitation over a given time. It consists of a funnel that directs rain into a calibrated bucket divided into two compartments. When a set amount of water fills one compartment, it tips, triggering a counter that records the rainfall. This mechanism allows for continuous monitoring and accurate data collection, making it a preferred choice for weather stations, agriculture, and hydrology.</p><p>The Tipping Bucket Rain Gauge Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for accurate weather data due to climate change and its impacts on agriculture, urban planning, and disaster management.
