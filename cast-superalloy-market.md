<p><p>The cast superalloy market is categorized into four primary types: iron base, nickel base, cobalt base, and others. Iron-based superalloys offer good strength at elevated temperatures and are often cost-effective. Nickel-based superalloys are known for their excellent high-temperature performance and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for aerospace and power generation applications. Cobalt-based superalloys provide superior wear resistance and stability under high temperatures, often utilized in gas turbines and military applications. The "other" category includes niche and specialized alloys for unique applications.</p></p>
