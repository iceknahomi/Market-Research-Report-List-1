<p><p>The Wi-Fi MCU chips market is segmented into single-band and dual-band categories. Single-band chips operate on either the 2.4 GHz or 5 GHz frequency, offering basic connectivity for simpler applications. In contrast, dual-band chips support both frequencies, allowing for greater flexibility and improved performance in environments with multiple devices. This versatility enables dual-band chips to handle higher data rates and reduced interference, making them suitable for more complex applications, such as smart home devices and IoT solutions.</p></p>
