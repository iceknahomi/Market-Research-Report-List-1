<p><strong>The Highly Transparent Low Iron Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Highly Transparent Low Iron Glass market encompasses two primary types: rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, creating a uniform thickness, ideal for applications requiring enhanced strength and durability. Float glass, on the other hand, is manufactured by floating molten glass on top of molten tin, resulting in a smooth, high-clarity surface. Both types are valued for their superior light transmission and low iron content, making them suitable for architectural and automotive applications.</p></p>
