<p><p>Quantum Well Infrared Photodetectors (QWIPs) are advanced semiconductor devices that detect infrared light using quantum wells to achieve high sensitivity and efficiency. The market for QWIPs is categorized into two main types: Light Guide Type and Photovoltaic Type. Light Guide QWIPs utilize optical fibers to enhance light collection and distribution, making them suitable for imaging applications. In contrast, Photovoltaic QWIPs convert infrared light directly into electrical signals, offering high responsivity and faster response times for diverse sensing applications.</p></p>
