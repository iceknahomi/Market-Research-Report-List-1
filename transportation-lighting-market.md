<p><p>The transportation lighting market consists of three key types: replacement installation, retrofit installation, and new installation. Replacement installation refers to the direct exchange of old lighting systems with new, improving efficiency and safety. Retrofit installation involves upgrading existing fixtures with modern technologies, enhancing performance without complete replacement. New installation entails the deployment of lighting systems in newly constructed transportation infrastructure, designed primarily for optimal energy efficiency and compliance with contemporary standards. Each segment addresses unique infrastructure and energy needs within transportation settings.</p></p>
