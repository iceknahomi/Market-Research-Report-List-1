<p><strong>The Flex Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flex Foam market is divided into two main types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell foam has a dense structure that traps air within individual cells, providing excellent buoyancy, insulation, and moisture resistance, making it ideal for applications in marine and construction industries. In contrast, open cell foam features interconnected cells that allow air and moisture to pass through, resulting in greater flexibility, breathability, and sound absorption, often used in furniture, bedding, and acoustic applications.</p></p>
