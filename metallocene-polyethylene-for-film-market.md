<p><p>Metallocene polyethylene (mPE) for film applications is categorized into two primary types based on melting index: high melting index (HMI) and low melting index (LMI) polyethylene. HMI mPE is characterized by improved strength, clarity, and processing efficiency, making it ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, LMI mPE offers enhanced flexibility and sealability, suited for packaging films that require lower processing temperatures. Both types leverage metallocene catalysts, resulting in superior molecular control and enhanced film properties for diverse applications.</p></p>
