<p><p>The Platinum-based Fuel Cell Catalysts market primarily includes two main types: Pt/C and Pt alloy catalysts. Pt/C consists of platinum nanoparticles dispersed on carbon supports, enhancing catalytic activity for fuel cell reactions. Pt alloy catalysts, on the other hand, incorporate other metals with platinum, improving performance by reducing platinum loading and enhancing resistance to poisoning. Both types are critical in optimizing fuel cell efficiency and durability, driving advancements in clean energy technologies and applications.</p></p>
