<p><p>The washroom ventilation fans market includes three primary types: ceiling fans, wall-mounted fans, and window-mounted fans. Ceiling fans are installed in the overhead space, effectively circulating air and reducing humidity levels. Wall-mounted fans are fixed to the wall, providing direct airflow and often featuring adjustable angles for targeted ventilation. Window-mounted fans are designed for installation in window openings, allowing for easy exhaust of stale air while bringing in fresh air from outside. Each type serves to enhance air quality and comfort in washrooms.</p></p>
