<p><p>The meter box market is divided primarily into two types: single-phase meter boxes and polyphase meter boxes. Single-phase meter boxes are designed for residential applications, handling lower voltage levels and catering to basic energy consumption needs. Conversely, polyphase meter boxes are used in commercial and industrial settings, accommodating higher voltage and multiple-phase systems to support greater energy demands. Both types ensure accurate energy measurement and facilitate efficient power distribution in their respective contexts.</p></p>
