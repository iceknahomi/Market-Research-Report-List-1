<p><p>Nitrile gloves are essential in various industries, including healthcare and food services, and are available in two main types: powdered and powder-free. Powdered nitrile gloves have a dusting of cornstarch, which facilitates easy wearing and removal. However, they can cause allergic reactions in some individuals. Powder-free nitrile gloves, on the other hand, are processed to eliminate powder, reducing the risk of irritation and contamination, making them the preferred choice for sensitive environments and those with allergies.</p></p>
