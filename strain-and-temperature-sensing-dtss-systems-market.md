<p><p>The Strain and Temperature Sensing (DTSS) Systems market is divided into two main types based on fiber types: Single Mode Fiber-Based DTSS and Multimode Fiber-Based DTSS. Single Mode Fiber-Based DTSS systems utilize a narrow core fiber, enabling long-distance signal transmission with minimal attenuation, making them suitable for precise measurements in critical applications. In contrast, Multimode Fiber-Based DTSS systems feature a larger core, allowing multiple light modes, thus supporting shorter distances and broader sensing areas, often used in less critical applications.</p></p>
